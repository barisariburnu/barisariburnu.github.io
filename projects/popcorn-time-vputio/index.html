Popcorn Time w/put.io

What is Popcorn Time?

It's a brilliant mashup that brings together several API's to provide a catalog for watching torrents.

What is Put.io?

Put.io is a paid service that you can delegate your torrenting to.

You know where we are going with this, right?

With "Popcorn Time w/put.io", instead of downloading torrents locally, you can utilize your put.io account. I have forked this application from the Popcorn Time Repo and I replaced torrent downloads with put.io API calls. Hope you like it
