Popcorn Time w/put.io

GETTING STARTED

What is Popcorn Time?

It's a brilliant mashup that brings together several API's to provide a catalog for watching torrents, that anybody can use.

What is Put.io?

Put.io is a service that you can delegate your torrenting to.

So, What's Popcorn Time w/put.io?

Instead of downloading torrents locally, you can utilize your put.io account. I have forked this application from the Popcornt Time Repository and I replaced torrent downloads with put.io API calls.
